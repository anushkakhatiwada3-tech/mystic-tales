✦ About Mystic Tales

A safe, magical space made for children and families

Our Mission

Mystic Tales was created with one simple belief — every child deserves a world of stories. We make it easy for children of all ages to discover enchanting tales, grow their imagination, and even become storytellers themselves.

🛡️

100% Safe

No ads, no logins required, no personal data collected from children.

📚

Age Appropriate

Every story is carefully crafted for its age group — from toddlers to tweens.

✨

Sparks Creativity

Kids don't just read — they create their own magical stories too.

For Parents & Teachers

  • ✓ No account or signup needed to read stories
  • ✓ Read aloud feature helps early readers follow along
  • ✓ Adjustable text size for comfortable reading
  • ✓ Stories filtered by age group so kids find the right fit
  • ✓ Create a Story feature encourages creative writing skills
